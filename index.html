About Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security. It operates independently of a central bank and can be transferred directly between users through a secure network.

Popular Cryptocurrencies

Bitcoin (BTC)

Bitcoin is the first decentralized cryptocurrency, created in 2009. It is often referred to as digital gold.

Ethereum (ETH)

Ethereum is a decentralized platform that allows developers to build smart contracts and decentralized applications.

Binance Coin (BNB)

Binance Coin is the cryptocurrency of the Binance exchange and is used to pay transaction fees on the platform.
